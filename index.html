How to use

  1. Fill form.
  2. Click on "Generate links" button.
  3. Click on "Open all links" button.

    4. * In order to open all links you must enable pop-ups for this site.

    * It's strongly recommended to read the section below.

Ideal workflow and useful keyboard shortcuts

  1. Once you click on the button "Open all links", go to the first tab created (the first tab from left to right).
  2. Whatsapp Web will open with the conversation and the message set.
  3. Send the message pressing Enter in your keyboard.
  4. When your message is sent (when the message status icon is one tick) press CTRL + W to close currently focused browser tab (Whatsapp Web).
  5. Your focus will change to the tab on the right (the next waiting room). Repeat from step 3.

    6. * No mouse is required at all, just your keyboard.

    * If you know programming, you can automate the process further with PyAutoGUI (Python) or Tampermonkey (Javascript).
